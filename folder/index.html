What is Global Campus VCN?

Our Network is a community of people who are interested in the educational industry: have relevant competencies and solid background, understand markets’ specifics, have connections with other learning enterprises, and have a desire to share their best practices. Depending on the field of the expertise, we can ask for your one-time consultation or offer to contribute to the existing/future project.

Domains

  • Academic Programs

    The EPAM School of Digital Engineering (ESDE) is a global digital educational project that combines our innovative technology expertise and academic experience to provide full-scale Bachelor’s and Master’s degree programs in software engineering. We cooperate with universities worldwide to offer a modern, flexible and practice-oriented curriculum.

  • Professional Education

    The current IT industry is as volatile and unpredictable as ever. The half-life of technical skills continues to shrink, while the time required to close the skills gap is growing. To stay competitive, we needed to bridge skills gaps fast. We offer a wide portfolio of learning solutions helping people start their career in the IT industry, as well as continuously grow their technical and soft skills to achieve professional goals.

  • Technology for Children, Youth and Seniors

    In a fun and safe environment, we encourage our younger generation to taste software engineering, develop skills and proficiencies, and become adepts of lifelong learning. Supervised by our experienced coders, our future engineers are gaining highly valuable IT skills, learning to become great problem solvers and grasp concepts faster which is the key to a strong and sustainable development of global IT community.

Roles of Education Contributors

Associate

Practitioners, of differing levels of seniority, with strong Subject Matter Expertise and hands-on experience in IT industry and strong motivation to participate in learning-related engagements. They possess less mature learning programs delivery background and/or skills and/or their reputation has not been confirmed.​​

Senior Engagement Orchestrator

  • Highly reputable senior learning professional with a proven track record of leading and orchestrating complex learning solutions engagements. ​​
  • Capable of maintaining senior level/executive relationships with clients/partners.​​
  • Responsible for the successful outcome of specific learning projects end-to-end.​​
  • Operates at different levels dependent on the size and complexity of the engagement.
  • Expected to gather and analyze data to continuously improve the value of the learning solutions offered to clients.​​

Practitioner

Practitioners, of differing levels of seniority, with strong Subject Matter Expertise and hands-on experience in IT industry and learning programs delivery background and/or skills - based upon confirmed reputation.​​

Senior Engagement Practitioner

  • Executive Level Learning Professional. Trusted Advisors within Learning Industry Domain.​​
  • Might play a role of individual contributor - but also capable of maintaining Senior Level/Executive relationships with Clients and educational Partners.​​

Senior Advisor

  • Highly reputable senior practitioners possessing the capabilities to understand - at a deep and broad level - the challenges and problems associated with complex engagements within the learning domain.​​
  • Capable of maintaining Senior Level/Executive Relationship with Clients and educational Partners.​​

Key principles of VCN formation

Some of you are already in the Network due to participation in different learning activities. Some of you would like to take part in educational projects. Some of you are just considering such a possibility – our Network is open to everyone.


The main principle of joining the Network is your wish to share knowledge, collaborate and embrace the best learning standards across EPAM.
